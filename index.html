📘 New Student Setup Guide (2026)

Welcome to Southwestern College! Everything you need to set up your new laptop and access your SC accounts — Microsoft 365, Blackboard, and Self-Service.

💻 Laptop Setup — Logging In for the First Time ▼
  1. Power on your laptop and complete the Windows setup wizard: select your language (English), region (United States), and keyboard layout (US), then skip the second keyboard layout.
  2. When prompted to connect to a network, connect to the SC_Secure network.
    Username: firstname.lastname@sckans.edu  ·  Password: your email password.
  3. Choose Yes if asked about being visible to other devices, then click Next.
  4. Sign into your laptop with your SC email address and password.
Already changed your Office 365 (email) password using the instructions sent to your personal email? Use that password to log in.

If this is the first time using your Office 365 account, you'll be prompted to set up additional security information to verify your identity. We recommend the Mobile App (Microsoft Authenticator) option for ease of use. You'll then be prompted to change your password. Your new password must be at least 8 characters and contain a capital letter, lowercase letter, number, and special character.

👤 Windows Hello Biometric Login ▼

All SC laptops include a Windows Hello facial recognition camera or fingerprint sensor for secure, password-free sign-in. You can enable it during initial setup (a 6-digit PIN is also required), or later:

  1. Type "Windows Hello" into the search bar next to the Start menu and choose Sign-in Options.
  2. Select Facial Recognition or Fingerprint Recognition, click Set up, and follow the prompts.
☁️ OneDrive Cloud Storage ▼

Every SC student gets OneDrive cloud storage (1TB by default — need more? submit a ticket). The SC Helpdesk automatically configures OneDrive to back up your Documents, Pictures, and Desktop folders. Check your sync status by clicking the OneDrive icon in your taskbar. More info: support.microsoft.com/onedrive.

📦 Windows Applications (Auto-Install) ▼

Your SC-issued laptop automatically downloads and installs Microsoft Office, Acrobat Reader, and the Cisco AnyConnect VPN client. This can take up to 15 minutes from first login — please stay connected to the network for about 15 minutes after logging in so everything installs.

🖨️ Printing on Campus (CardSwipe) ▼
  1. Email your document to cardswipe@sckans.edu from your SC email address.
  2. Go to any CardSwipe printer on campus and swipe your student ID card to retrieve your print job.

Supported formats: Word (.doc/.docx), Excel (.xls/.xlsx), PowerPoint (.ppt/.pptx), PDF, and common image files (.jpg, .gif, .png).

📧 Microsoft 365 — Email & Office ▼

SC provides all students with a Microsoft 365 account for the duration of enrollment — SC email, online Office apps, OneDrive cloud storage, and more.

To log in, go to office.sckans.edu and sign in with your full email address (firstname.lastname@sckans.edu) and password.

Mobile setup: iOS instructions · Android instructions · Microsoft 365 Policy

📚 Blackboard — First Login & Password ▼

Go to learn.sckans.edu and sign in with your username (student ID number without dashes or spaces) and the password Jinx#### (#### = last 4 digits of your ID number).

To change your password after logging in: open the drop-down menu by your name (upper-right) → Settings → Personal Information → Change Password, enter your current password and your new password twice, then submit.

🗂️ Self-Service — First Login & Password Reset ▼

Go to selfservice.sckans.edu and sign in with your username (student ID number without dashes or spaces) and password.

First time, or forgot your password? Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the Self-Service homepage. You'll receive an email from do_not_reply@sckans.edu at your primary email on file (typically your SC email) with a link to set a new password.

📱 Set Up Microsoft Teams on Your Phone ▼
  1. Find the app: Search "Microsoft Teams" in the Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iPhone).
  2. Download: Tap Install or Get, then Open when it finishes.
  3. Sign in: Enter your school email address and password.
  4. Choose account: If asked, pick Work or school account (not Personal).

Schools often use a special login screen — you may need to re-enter your password or approve a login code on another device. This is normal. Once you're in, you can chat with the Helpdesk at sc.helpdesk.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my SC password? ▼
Visit the Password Reset Portal and follow the prompts. You'll need access to your recovery email, phone, or Authenticator app. If you're locked out, contact the Helpdesk at 620-229-6444.
What software do I get for free? ▼
All enrolled students receive Microsoft 365 (email, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, and 1TB of OneDrive storage) at no cost for the duration of enrollment. SC-issued laptops also auto-install Office, Acrobat Reader, and the Cisco AnyConnect VPN client.
How do I connect to SC campus Wi-Fi? ▼
Select SC_Secure from your network list and sign in with your SC email address (firstname.lastname@sckans.edu) and password. See the Wi-Fi setup guide for device-specific instructions.
How do I print on campus? ▼
Email your document to cardswipe@sckans.edu from your SC email, then swipe your student ID at any CardSwipe printer on campus to release your print job.
My Blackboard isn't loading — what do I do? ▼
Try clearing your browser cache and signing in again at learn.sckans.edu. During business hours, contact the Helpdesk; for after-hours Blackboard issues, call 316-201-3490 (available 24/7).
Can I buy a used SC laptop? ▼
Yes — SC resells laptops at the end of their cycle. Visit the Laptop Resales form to see availability and purchase.

🛡️ Security & Acceptable Use

Protect your SC account and personal data with these essential practices.

🔐 Set up Multi-Factor Authentication with the Microsoft Authenticator app — you'll be prompted at first login, or manage it anytime at password.sckans.edu.
🎣 Never share your SC password. IT will never ask for it via email or phone.
🔑 Passwords must be 8+ characters with a capital letter, lowercase letter, number, and special character.
📱 Keep your devices and software updated, and review the Microsoft 365 Policy before using campus technology resources.
