❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my SC password? ▼
Visit the Password Reset Portal and follow the prompts. You'll need access to your recovery email or phone number. If locked out, contact the helpdesk at 620-229-6444.
What software do I get for free? ▼
All enrolled students receive Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, OneDrive) at no cost. Additional software is available in computer labs.
How do I connect to SC campus Wi-Fi? ▼
Select "SC-Wireless" from your network list and sign in with your SC credentials. Visit our Wi-Fi setup guide for device-specific instructions.
My Blackboard isn't loading — what do I do? ▼
First, check the System Status page. If all systems are green, try clearing your browser cache. For after-hours issues, call 316-201-3490.
Can I borrow a laptop? ▼
Yes. Short-term loaner laptops are available through the Helpdesk. Visit the Helpdesk in Christy Admin during business hours to check availability.
